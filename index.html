What's the Turtle's Idea?

Turtles' idea is to start a global movement to reduce plastic straw usage and help clean up the oceans. The turtles suggest we start utilizing environmentally friendly reusable bamboo straws.

We are attempting to tackle a global problem by reducing the unimaginably high amount of plastic straws used in the world. In the US alone, more than 500 million plastic straws are being used every single day. A lot of those end up in the ocean and damage the marine world.

Reusability

We help the sea turtles by providing reusable straws - made of bamboo. We are attempting to reduce the unimaginably high amount of plastic straw usage in the world. Vast amounts of plastic straws end up in the oceans and seas. This severely damages and endangers the marine ecosystems and their creatures like sea turtles, fish, dolphins and whales, seals, and sea birds.

Quality

Will you chose to help the world & be the most aesthetic coffee drinker in Starbucks? Purchase the Turtles' Idea bamboo straws and help us to start a global movement!

Impact

For every purchase, we donate 50 Euro cents to SeaLifeTrust.org charity, which is making the oceans more healthy, properly protected and full of diverse life (yes, including sea turtles - the stars of Turtles' Idea).
